- Added votes table to Convex schema (convex/schema.ts:40-47) with fields: gameCode, voterId, votedForId, submittedAt
- Created indexes: by_game (gameCode) and by_voter (gameCode, voterId) for efficient querying
- Added submitVote mutation (convex/games.ts:889-963) with comprehensive validation:
